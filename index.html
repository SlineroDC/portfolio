Marvel World

My favorite characters from the Marvel universe are: Spiderman, Hulk and Iron Man.

Spiderman

superhero known for his web-slinging abilities and superhuman strength. He can stick to walls, leap great distances, and has enhanced agility and stamina.

Hulk

The Hulk is a fictional superhero from Marvel Comics and the Marvel Cinematic Universe, portrayed as a green, giant-sized being with superhuman strength and durability.

Iron Man

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a fictional superhero in the Marvel Comics universe. He's a brilliant inventor, billionaire, and philanthropist who wears a high-tech, armored suit to fight crime and protect the world.
